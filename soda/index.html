SODA is the first solo exhibition of katya. it is generally an expanded display of personal artistic work. 198 one-copy original pieces are split in three general groups:

// paintings in acrylic — which require no artist’s background acknowledge.

// a series called UPD — an initial drawing + a new form of the depicted, like a sculpture or a photo.

// sketches in ink — small sized pieces drawn straight in (without a pencil pre-sketch), katya’s way of coming up with bigger ideas.


soda is katya’s oriental shorthair cat. Soda the name is a mispronounced led zeppelin’s album Coda.


took place august 2024 in moscow, carried out solely by katya (with the help of friends)

cartoons are a way of delivering a story: a progressive narrative or a thread of subjects, the main point is unfolding an idea. collaborating with music artists has been a delightful chance to blend cartoon plots with a different medium of sound.

  • mooden the cat

    large-scale animation started with mooden the cat. a cartoon happened to be an appropriate way of telling a story about the character. in the video his name is explained, his habits & leisure described.

  • waiters — music video

    single from mad meg’s alum ‘who deserves balloons & medals’. the initial idea behind the video was bits & pieces of references, many of those based on gestures to mad meg discography & the lyrics.

  • when love turns

    music video for single from sons of venus album ‘crisis to crisis’. animated visual narrative relies on the lyrics & love. the idea for the video is a complicated emotion: a feeling of being frustrated & damaged, yet finding the strength to somehow manage.

  • the horse is not mine

    “the horse is not mine” is named after an old russian idiom to deny one’s responsibility in a situation that goes, “i am not me, and the horse is not mine.”

    single by Ilya Popenko

    video by Mykola Metin
